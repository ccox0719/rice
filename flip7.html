Flip 7 — Official Rules

Deck: 94 cards. Numbers 0–12 where count equals value (twelve 12s, one 1, one 0), plus Score Modifiers (+2,+4,+6,+8,+8,+10,×2) and Action cards.

Each Round: The dealer deals one card face-up to every player. Action cards are resolved immediately. Then clockwise — each player chooses to Flip (draw a card) or Bank (lock score and sit out).

Bust: Draw a duplicate number = 0 points for the round.

Flip 7: Collect 7 different numbers → round ends for all. Everyone who hasn't busted banks. Flip 7 player gets +15 bonus.

Action Cards:

  • Freeze — Forces one active player to bank immediately.
  • Flip Three — Forces one active player to flip 3 more cards.
  • Second Chance — Held in hand. Saves you from one bust.

Score Modifiers: +cards add flat points; ×2 doubles your number total, then add bonus cards.

Win: First to 200 points wins.
